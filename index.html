Warning Signs

Signs That Can't Wait

Not every plumbing issue is an emergency, but some demand immediate attention. A burst or leaking pipe can flood a room within minutes. No water or no hot water throughout the house often points to a major line or heater failure. In older Bronx homes across Tremont and Kingsbridge, aging pipes make these issues more common.

  • Burst or actively leaking pipes
  • No water or hot water at all
  • Sewage backing up into sinks or tubs
  • Gas smell near the water heater
  • Flooding around fixtures or appliances
Notice any of these? Don't wait it out — shut off your main water valve if you can, and call our team right away.

Signs That Can Usually Wait

Some issues are annoying but not urgent. A slow drain, a dripping faucet, or a running toilet can typically wait for a scheduled appointment. That said, in Mott Haven and Morris Park homes, minor issues tend to worsen quickly with aging galvanized pipes — worth a call sooner rather than later.

  • Slow-draining sink or tub
  • A dripping faucet
  • A toilet that runs occasionally
  • Minor water pressure changes
